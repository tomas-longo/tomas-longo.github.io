Onis

  • Strategy
  • Local Multiplayer
  • Cubic world
  • Azulejo art

Onis is a strategy-turn-based game with multiplayer features!
You will enter a cubic world with the goal of stealing the opponent's crown and returning it to your home base.
Trying to be faster than the opponent can be a good strategy, but you will also need to defend your base.
With the help of power-ups, you can hamper the opponent or get the upper hand.
If you are up for it, you can also drown your opponent and make it return to its base.
